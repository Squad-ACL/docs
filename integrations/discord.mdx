This guide will help you get the Squad ACL Discord bot invited to your server and connected to your Squad ACL organization.

### Before you begin

- Must be a Squad ACL partner.
- Must be an owner or admin of your organization.
- Must have Discord Administrator access.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Invite the Discord bot">
    To be able to link the Squad ACL Discord bot, it must be in your Discord server. To invite the bot, you must go to **Settings → Discord** and click '_Invite Bot_'. From there, select your community Discord and agree to the permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your Link code">
    To link your Discord server to your Squad ACL organization, you must go to **Settings → Discord** and click '_Link Bot_'. This will copy a linking code to your clipboard. If it does not, a notification will appear with your code. Copy it from there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link the bot">
    Open your Discord server and use the command `/organization-link` and put your code into the 'link' field. If your organization is linked, you should see a confirmation of this. You can also check on the **Settings → Discord** page by refreshing and the buttons should be grayed out.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Card title="Issues Linking" type="tip">
  If you have any issues with linking your Discord community to your ACL organization. Please contact support and we will be able to further assist you in linking.
</Card>

### Discord Commands

- `/generate-seed-embed` This command will generate a seed embed where members of your Discord can check their progress and link their Steam account to their Discord account.
- `/seed-leaderboard` This command will show the current top 10 seeders. _At this time, this command only shows the top 10, there will be support for more in the future._
